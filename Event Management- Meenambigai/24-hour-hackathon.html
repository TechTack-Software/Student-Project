24-Hour Hackathon 2026

24-Hour Hackathon 2026 is an exciting coding marathon designed for passionate developers, designers, and innovators who love solving real-world problems through technology. This non-stop innovation challenge brings together creative minds to build unique projects, applications, and smart solutions within just 24 hours.

Participants will work individually or in teams to develop innovative ideas in areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, Mobile Applications, Cyber Security, Healthcare Technology, and Smart Automation. Throughout the event, mentors and industry experts will guide participants with technical support, problem-solving strategies, and project development tips.

The hackathon creates a highly energetic and collaborative environment where creativity, teamwork, and coding skills come together. From brainstorming sessions and coding challenges to final project presentations, 24-Hour Hackathon 2026 promises an unforgettable experience for every participant.

Event Highlights

24-hour nonstop coding and innovation challenge
Mentorship sessions from industry professionals
Exciting prizes for winning teams and projects
Project presentations and startup idea pitching
Networking opportunities with developers and innovators
Free food, refreshments, and rest zones for participants
Hands-on learning and real-world problem solving experience

Who Can Participate

24-Hour Hackathon 2026 is open to students, programmers, designers, developers, and technology enthusiasts who are passionate about coding and innovation. Participants from Computer Science, IT, Electronics, and Engineering backgrounds are especially encouraged to participate.

Both beginners and experienced developers can join individually or as teams. This event is an excellent opportunity to improve technical skills, collaborate with others, and transform creative ideas into working solutions.

What to Bring

Participants should bring their college ID card or registration confirmation for entry verification. Teams are advised to carry laptops, chargers, extension boards, headphones, and any required software or hardware accessories for project development.

Comfortable clothing is recommended since the event runs continuously for 24 hours. Participants may also carry notebooks, water bottles, and personal essentials to stay comfortable throughout the hackathon.
