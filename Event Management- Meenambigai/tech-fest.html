Tech Fest 2026

Tech Fest 2026 is one of the biggest and most exciting technology events of the year, bringing together students, developers, innovators, and tech enthusiasts from various colleges and institutions. The event is designed to inspire creativity, encourage innovation, and provide hands-on learning experiences through competitions, workshops, and live demonstrations.

Participants will get the opportunity to explore the latest trends in Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, Robotics, Cyber Security, App Development, and Digital Innovation. The fest also includes interactive sessions with industry experts, startup founders, and experienced developers who will share their knowledge and real-world experiences.

From exciting coding challenges and hackathons to project exhibitions and gaming zones, Tech Fest 2026 promises a fun-filled learning environment for every participant. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced programmer, this event will help you improve your skills, build connections, and discover new career opportunities in the world of technology.

Event Highlights

24-Hour Hackathon with exciting cash prizes
Live coding competition and debugging challenge
AI, Robotics, and IoT project exhibitions
Workshops on Web Development and Cyber Security
Guest lectures from industry professionals
Startup networking and career guidance sessions
Gaming arena and interactive tech experience zones

Who Can Participate

Tech Fest 2026 is open to all college students, school students, tech enthusiasts, and aspiring developers who are passionate about technology and innovation. Participants from Computer Science, IT, Electronics, Mechanical, and other departments are welcome to join the competitions and workshops.

Both individual and team participation are allowed for selected events. Beginners who want to explore technology and experienced participants who want to showcase their talents can equally benefit from this exciting fest.

What to Bring

Participants are requested to carry their college or school ID card for verification. Those attending workshops or hackathons should bring their laptops, chargers, and necessary accessories. Internet facilities, seating arrangements, and technical support will be provided by the organizers.

Comfortable clothing is recommended as the event includes multiple activities throughout the day. Participants are also encouraged to carry notebooks for taking notes during technical sessions and workshops.
