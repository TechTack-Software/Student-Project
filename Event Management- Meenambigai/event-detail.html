Tech Fest 2026

Tech Fest 2026 brings together students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts for a day of discovery. Join immersive workshops, product demos, hackathons, and inspiring talks from industry leaders. This annual event is designed to showcase innovative projects, foster collaboration, and create memorable experiences for all participants.

The event features various activities including live coding competitions, project exhibitions, networking sessions, and interactive workshops. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn new skills or an expert wanting to share your knowledge, Tech Fest 2026 offers something for everyone.

Event Highlights

Coding contests with prizes worth ₹50,000
Merit-based scholarship announcements
Interactive learning workshops and demo booths
Career guidance and counseling sessions
Project showcase and networking opportunities
Mid-day meal for all participants
Sports and games activities

Who Can Participate

This event is open to all students from government schools and underprivileged backgrounds across the region. Students from classes 6th to 12th are especially encouraged to participate and benefit from this program. This is a great opportunity to connect with mentors, learn new skills, and explore career opportunities in the technology sector.

What to Bring

Participants should bring their school ID card, a notebook for workshops, and any previous academic records if applying for scholarships. All other materials including books, stationery, and meals will be provided by the organizers. Comfortable clothing and shoes are recommended as there will be various activities throughout the day.
