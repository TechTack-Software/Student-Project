AI & Robotics Expo 2026

AI & Robotics Expo 2026 is a futuristic technology event designed to showcase the latest innovations in Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Automation, and Smart Technologies. The expo brings together students, developers, engineers, researchers, and technology enthusiasts to explore cutting-edge advancements shaping the future.

The event features interactive robot demonstrations, AI-powered applications, automation systems, and live technology showcases from talented students and industry professionals. Participants will gain hands-on experience through workshops, seminars, coding sessions, and innovation challenges focused on real-world technology solutions.

From intelligent robots and machine learning projects to smart home systems and futuristic inventions, AI & Robotics Expo 2026 promises an exciting learning experience filled with creativity, innovation, and discovery. The event encourages participants to think beyond limits and explore the endless possibilities of modern technology.

Event Highlights

Live AI and robotics project demonstrations
Hands-on workshops on Machine Learning and Automation
Robotics competition with exciting prizes
Interactive sessions with technology experts
AI-powered gaming and virtual reality experience zones
Startup innovation and smart tech exhibitions
Networking opportunities with developers and engineers

Who Can Participate

AI & Robotics Expo 2026 is open to students, developers, engineers, innovators, and technology enthusiasts interested in Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and Smart Technologies. Participants from Computer Science, IT, Electronics, Robotics, Mechanical, and Engineering departments are encouraged to join.

Both beginners and experienced innovators can participate in workshops, project showcases, coding competitions, and robotics challenges. This event provides an excellent platform to learn, collaborate, and showcase creative technological ideas.

What to Bring

Participants should carry their college or school ID card for entry verification. Those participating in competitions or exhibitions are advised to bring their laptops, robotic kits, project materials, chargers, and required technical accessories.

Comfortable clothing is recommended as the event includes multiple workshops and live activities throughout the day. Participants are also encouraged to carry notebooks for taking notes during seminars and technical sessions.
