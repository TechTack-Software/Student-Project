About MediCare Hospital

MediCare Hospital is a modern healthcare institution dedicated to providing quality medical services and patient care. The hospital was established in the year 2015 with the aim of offering affordable and advanced healthcare facilities to people. The hospital was founded by Dr. R. Chakaravarthi, a well-known medical professional who wanted to create a patient-friendly healthcare center with modern medical technology and experienced doctors.
