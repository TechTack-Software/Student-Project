About Kashmir

Kashmir is one of India's most beautiful tourist destinations. Famous for snow-covered mountains, Dal Lake, gardens and valleys, Kashmir offers unforgettable travel experiences for nature lovers. Visitors can enjoy shikara rides, delicious Kashmiri food and peaceful landscapes throughout the year.

Top Attractions

Dal Lake

Dal Lake is one of Kashmir's most beautiful attractions, famous for its peaceful shikara rides and floating markets. Visitors can enjoy breathtaking mountain views, houseboats and the calm beauty of the sparkling lake.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg is a popular hill station known for snowfall, skiing and adventure activities. Tourists visit this beautiful destination to enjoy snow-covered mountains, cable car rides and breathtaking winter scenery.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam is a peaceful valley surrounded by rivers, pine forests and scenic mountain landscapes. Visitors can enjoy horse riding, nature walks and relaxing moments in the cool and refreshing climate.
