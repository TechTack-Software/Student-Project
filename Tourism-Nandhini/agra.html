About Agra

Agra is one of India's most famous historical cities. It is home to the iconic Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Agra showcases beautiful Mughal architecture, royal palaces and ancient heritage that attracts tourists from all over the world.

Top Attractions

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the world's most famous monuments, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of Mumtaz Mahal. Visitors admire its beautiful white marble architecture, romantic history and stunning sunrise and sunset views.

Agra Fort

Agra Fort is a magnificent Mughal fort known for its royal palaces, massive red sandstone walls and rich history. Tourists can explore ancient halls, gardens and beautiful views of the Taj Mahal from inside the fort.

Mehtab Bagh

Mehtab Bagh is a peaceful garden located near the Yamuna River, offering breathtaking sunset views of the Taj Mahal. Visitors enjoy photography, greenery and a calm atmosphere away from the busy city crowd.
