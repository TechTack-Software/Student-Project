About Valparai

Valparai is a hidden hill station in Tamil Nadu surrounded by tea plantations, forests and wildlife. It is perfect for peace lovers and nature explorers.

Top Attractions

Tea Estates

Valparai is famous for its vast green tea estates surrounded by misty hills and cool climate. Visitors can enjoy peaceful nature walks, scenic viewpoints and the refreshing beauty of the Western Ghats.

Monkey Falls

Monkey Falls is a beautiful natural waterfall located near Valparai, attracting tourists with its cool water and lush green surroundings. It is a perfect place for relaxation, photography and enjoying nature.

Aliyar Dam

Aliyar Dam is a scenic tourist attraction surrounded by mountains and greenery. Visitors can enjoy peaceful lake views, picnic spots and beautiful sunset scenery near the calm waters of the dam.
