About Ooty

Ooty is one of South India's most famous hill stations. It is known for cool climate, green valleys, tea gardens, lakes and mountain views. Tourists visit Ooty to relax, enjoy nature and experience peaceful hill station beauty.

Top Attractions

Tea Gardens

Ooty's tea gardens are famous for their endless green plantations, fresh mountain air and peaceful atmosphere. Visitors can walk through scenic tea estates, enjoy beautiful hill views and taste fresh homemade tea.

Ooty Lake

Ooty Lake is a popular tourist attraction surrounded by eucalyptus trees and natural beauty. Tourists can enjoy boating, cycling and relaxing moments with family and friends in the cool hill station climate.

Doddabetta Peak

Doddabetta Peak is the highest mountain peak in Ooty, offering breathtaking panoramic views of valleys and forests. Visitors love the cool weather, photography spots and beautiful sunrise experiences from the hilltop.
