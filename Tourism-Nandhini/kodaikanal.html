About Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is one of the most popular hill stations in Tamil Nadu, known for its cool climate, misty hills and scenic lake views.

Top Attractions

Kodaikanal Lake

Kodaikanal Lake is one of the most popular attractions in the hill station, famous for its peaceful surroundings and boating activities. Visitors can enjoy cycling, horse riding and relaxing moments in the cool climate.

Coaker's Walk

Coaker's Walk is a scenic walking pathway offering stunning views of valleys, hills and clouds. Tourists enjoy peaceful nature walks, cool weather and breathtaking sunrise and sunset views from this viewpoint.

Pillar Rocks

Pillar Rocks is a famous tourist spot known for its giant rock formations surrounded by misty mountains and greenery. Visitors love photography, scenic landscapes and the calm atmosphere of this natural attraction.
