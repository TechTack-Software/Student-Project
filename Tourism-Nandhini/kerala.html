About Kerala

Kerala is one of the most beautiful tourist destinations in India. It is famous for its backwaters, tea plantations, houseboats, beaches and traditional culture. Millions of tourists visit Kerala every year to enjoy nature and peaceful vacations.

Top Tourist Places

Alleppey

Alleppey is one of Kerala's most beautiful tourist destinations, famous for its peaceful backwaters, luxury houseboats, green coconut trees and village life. Visitors can enjoy boat rides, delicious Kerala food and stunning sunset views. It is the perfect place for relaxation and nature lovers.

Munnar

Munnar is a hill station surrounded by tea plantations, misty mountains and cool weather. It is a perfect place for honeymoon trips, nature photography and peaceful vacations with scenic viewpoints and waterfalls.

Kovalam

Kovalam is a popular beach destination known for its golden sands, lighthouse beach and water activities. Visitors can relax near the sea, enjoy seafood and experience the beauty of Kerala's coastal culture.

Traditional Kerala Food

Kerala is also famous for delicious traditional foods like Appam, Puttu, Kerala Fish Curry and Banana Chips. Kerala cuisine is rich in coconut flavors and spices.
