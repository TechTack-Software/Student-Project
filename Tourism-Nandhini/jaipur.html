About Jaipur

Jaipur is the capital city of Rajasthan and is famous for royal heritage, palaces and traditional culture. The city attracts tourists with its colorful architecture, delicious food and historical monuments.

Top Attractions

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal is Jaipur's most iconic palace famous for its beautiful pink sandstone design and hundreds of small windows. Visitors admire its unique architecture, royal history and stunning views of the colorful Pink City.

Amber Fort

Amber Fort is a magnificent royal fort known for its grand palaces, artistic carvings and rich Rajasthan history. Tourists enjoy elephant rides, traditional architecture and beautiful hilltop views surrounding the fort.

City Palace

City Palace is a historic royal residence featuring museums, courtyards and luxurious palace interiors. Visitors can explore royal collections, traditional costumes and the beautiful blend of Mughal and Rajput architecture.
